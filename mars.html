MAVERICK COLLECTION

I collect Key Forge decks with maverick cards IN house MARS.

“What is a Maverick?”

In Key Forge, a maverick is an extremely rare instance of a card that has left its standard house and is now a part of a new house. Like getting a Saurian dinosaur card in house Star Alliance.

Frequently these errant cards fizzle or do nothing. Sometimes they create quirkly little jank scenarios. Sometimes they set up an actual synergy that is not otherwise possible in-house.

“How can I tell if my deck has a maverick?”

You will see the maverick symbol in the upper right corner of the card. You will also see the maverick symbol next to the card name on the archon card.
