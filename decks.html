FAQ

What is this?

My personal collection of keyforge (and other game) deck hijinx.

Why?

Look we all love sites like deckbox.org or whatever the flavor of the month is but I wanted to make my own site that is a hybrid of blog, deck organization, and programming playground. I've been playing long enough to see sites and services retire and or have their dns stop resolving. If anyone is going to let my data go corrupt it's me!
