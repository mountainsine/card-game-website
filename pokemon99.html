Pokémon 99

This a fan-made Pokémon format.

In Pokémon 99 you build decks with the cards and play with the rules that existed for the Pokémon Trading card game in 99 when it was released.

HOW TO PLAY

TO PLAY YOU WILL NEED

  • Pokémon 99 Cube (see Instructions below for budget approach)
  • 10-30 min for deckbuilding
  • 45 min for game.
  • Basic Understanding of Pokémon.

HOW TO PLAY WITH PROXIES

YOU WILL NEED:

  • Black and White Printer (HP neverstop. I already had this.)
  • 200+ Penny Sleeves (Beckett Shield Clear Soft Card Sleeves $10 amazon)
  • 100+ Blank Playing Cards (Apostrophe Games Blank Playing Cards (Poker Size & Matte Finish) 2.5" x 3.5" $20 amazon)
  • Guillotine Cutter (Swingline Paper Cutter, Guillotine Trimmer, 15" Cut Length, ClassicCut Lite (9315) $35 amazon).
