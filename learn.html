With gameplay motifs, strategies, and categorizations.....as with many things KEYFORGE...frequently decks will become a blend of these ideas and card focus points.

🔥 ARCHON FORMAT

Similar to "standard" in magic the gathering"

In general "spike" or competitive players are drawn to this format.

Keyforge is weird though...because of the lack of deckbuilding and the scarcity/opinionated-ness around what makes a strong deck or player...some spike players may actually gravitate towards more intense formats like reversal or adapative!

🧠 ADAPTIVE FORMAT

A more skill intensive iteration with chain bidding based on outcomes.

🙃 REVERSAL FORMAT

Bring your worst deck and your opponent must play it against you.

Here are some cards related to the REVERSAL format!

There are many other formats as well including some fan created ones like Adaptive-Short (Adaptive with only 1 round)

🧩 COMBO ARCHETYPE

Put the cards together and win! These decks focus on 2-3 card combos or clear synergies for a win condition.

MID RANGE

A solid performer that may is not toptier but also not "not-competitive"

A skilled player could go 4/4 with this type of deck in a sealed or casual environment though!

Within this archetype you'll find the majority of keyforge deck lists.

GROUP HUG

The primary goal of a group hug deck is to make sure everyone gets to have fun and do cool things with their deck. Usually with a focus on symmetrical benefits that lead into sneaky win condition.

You can see the influence from MAGIC THE GATHERING here not so subtly
