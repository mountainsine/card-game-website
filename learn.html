Formats

🔥 ARCHON

Similar to "standard" in magic the gathering"

In general "spike" or competitive players are drawn to this format.

Keyforge is weird though...because of the lack of deckbuilding and the scarcity/opinionated-ness around what makes a strong deck or player...some spike players may actually gravitate towards more intense formats like reversal or adapative!

🧠 ADAPTIVE

A more skill intensive iteration with chain bidding based on outcomes.

🙃 REVERSAL

Bring your worst deck and your opponent must play it against you.

Archetypes

    🧩 Combo

Put the cards together and play to the lines.


    🛑 Control

Control the Game. Disrupt the tempo.


    🚀 Rush

Make all other strategies irrelevant by simply being faster!
