JANK TOWN

If you love the quirkiness and chaos of JANK decks then keyforge is an idea playground for you as a player. Initially, I was categorizing many of these type of keyforge decks as "Collectible."...since they did not fit in the competitive tiering space.

This became unmanage-able over time and I've transitioned to thinking as "Collectible" more for alt art decks, more special decks, etc.

On this page you'll find my junk drawer of....the rest. Weird decks, decks with combos that pay out little, decks with engines that go nowhere, strange names, and more!

JANK is more than the lack of amber control.
