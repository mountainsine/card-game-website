JANK TOWN

I LOVE the quirkiness and chaos of jank decks so I initially was categorizing many of them as "Collectible."

This became unmanage-able over time and I've transitioned to thinking as "Collectible" more for alt art decks, more special decks, etc.

On this page you'll find my junk drawer of....the rest. Weird decks, decks with combos that pay out little, decks with engines that go nowhere, strange names, and more!
