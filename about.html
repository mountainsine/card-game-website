ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

This site is a 💚 love 💚 letter for KEYFORGE, trading card games, and the outsiders who play them.

A lot of the card game websites and online tools are:

  • ❌ Riddled with ANALYTICS 🤮
  • ❌ Based around a procedural scoring engine and/or approach to a game with luck 🍀 and human interaction 🤖
  • ❌ Designed very poorly...or in a way that is tacky (ads, garish colors, etc.)

I wanted to create a website that offered an alternative perspective on the game and housed some of my favorite ideas and art.
